# Mentorship Program: Role Research
As part of the mentorship program, we explore the core requirements organizations prioritize when recruiting—skills, experience, certifications, and education—because these elements not only demonstrate a candidate’s ability to perform but also signal readiness for growth and alignment with industry standards. Understanding how these factors influence hiring decisions helps mentors guide participants toward building competitive profiles that stand out in today’s talent-driven market. <BR /> 

## Our goal for this role research:<BR /> 
Research security roles to determine required skills, certifications, experience, and educational requirements to provide visibility, in the current job landscape.<BR /> 

## Frequency of this research:<BR /> 
Annual research per role<BR /> 

## How is the research completed:<BR /> 
Researcher will obtain 30-50 JDs and review against expected criteria.<BR /> 
The greater the number of roles diseminated the greater the accuracy of the role research report.<BR /> 

## What roles we intend to research:
* Security Analyst
* Security Engineer
* Security Manager
* Security Architect
* Security Director


## What specifically are employers looking for:
* Skills
* Certifications
* Experience
* Education
